Why You Should Choose Clean Freak Cleaning Services

A quick online search will display numerous options for a San Diego house cleaning company, so you might be wondering what makes Clean Freak Cleaning Services better than the rest. We believe that it is not just one thing that distinguishes our home and business cleaning service from all of the others – it is a combination of what we have to offer. Our cleaning company offers detailed and affordable housekeeping services provided by professional house cleaners combined with top-notch customer service and a desire to help you enjoy the fact that you live in America’s finest city.

DETAIL-ORIENTED CLEANING At Clean Freak Cleaning Services, we take our name seriously. Our cleaners are meticulous in all of their work with an attention to detail that helps us shine above the competition. Whether you get our basic house cleaning services or our commercial cleaning services, you will benefit from our detailed cleaning checklist. If you need an even deeper cleaning than our regular services, you will truly benefit from our home detailing service – it’s that extra level clean that is perfect for spring cleaning or moving into or out of a new home.

AFFORDABLE, LUXURY CLEANING SERVICES Our cleaning company has created a luxury service that anyone can afford. For a low, hourly rate you can get your home or business the professional clean it needs. Don’t pay an arm and a leg for run-of-the-mill cleaners when you can get a deep cleaning at an affordable rate. The cost of our cleaning service includes not only the work of the house cleaner, but also the use of our cleaning supplies and equipment.

EXCELLENT CUSTOMER SERVICE We are proud of the level of customer service we offer. During Clean Freak’s business hours, there is always a professional available to answer your phone calls. After-hours voicemails and emails to our cleaning company are followed up in a timely manner.

Customer service goes beyond just getting your business; we want to stay your housekeepers. Because of commitment to customer satisfaction, at Clean Freak Cleaning Services, we follow up with our customers after each cleaning service and ask for feedback. We want to know if our maid service met expectations and, if not, we want to know what we could do better next time. Unlike other cleaning companies, our job isn’t done until we know every single customer is happy and satisfied with us. We will go out of our way to accommodate someone if a customer is not satisfied. We want to ensure that we are providing a service that stands up to our name.

PROFESSIONAL HOUSE AND OFFICE CLEANERS Sometimes you may find that you need to give up a level of professionalism to get affordable housekeeping services. That is not the case at Clean Freak Cleaning Services. Our home and office cleaners are always uniformed, speak English, and are very friendly. This not only provides greater efficiency in the long run, as the cleaner will be familiar with your home and your personal needs, but it will also instill you with an added level of comfort and security since you will be familiar with the cleaner in your home.

Residential

On each house cleaning visit, our professional maids follow a cleaning checklist to make sure we give you the spotless home you deserve. Our basic residential cleaning services include:

KITCHEN CLEANING

When cleaning your kitchen, our professional house cleaners will:

Degrease stove

Clean exterior and interior of microwave

Clean exterior of dishwasher

Clean exterior of refrigerator

Clean appliances

Clean countertops

Wipe down cabinet doors

Dust light fixtures

Clean windows

Clean ceiling fans

Sweep / Mop

Remove trash and replace trash bags

BATHROOM CLEANING

Each bathroom in your home will receive a thorough cleaning. Some of the main cleaning services our maids will complete include:

Clean sink and mirrors

Clean shower and shower door / tub

Clean and disinfect toilet

Wipe down cabinet doors

Dust light fixtures

Sweep / Mop

Remove trash and replace trash bags

BEDROOMS AND OTHER LIVING AREAS

The bedrooms and other living areas in your home (living room, dining room, den, family room, etc.) will also receive a thorough cleaning. In all rooms of your home, our maid service includes:

Removal of trash and replacement of trash bags

Sweeping / Mopping

Dusting of light fixtures and ceiling fans

Dusting and cleaning of knickknacks, wall hangings, and other objects in the room

Cleaning of doors, including removal of fingerprints

Cleaning of windows

Polishing of furniture

Making of bed

Cleaning of baseboards

Vacuuming
